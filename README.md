Retail Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales and customer behavior using Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to identify business trends, profitability drivers, customer purchasing patterns, and regional performance.

The project follows a complete end-to-end analytics workflow from raw data cleaning to interactive business intelligence dashboarding.

🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data, making it difficult to identify:

Revenue-driving product categories
High-value customers
Profitability trends
Regional sales performance
Impact of discounts on profit margins

This project helps transform raw sales data into actionable business insights.
